About Be Clean

Born from a passion for protecting our planet and the creatures we share it with

Our company was founded on the belief that effective cleaning shouldn't come at the cost of our environment or animal welfare. In a world where many brands still test on animals and use harmful chemicals, we saw the urgent need for change.

At Be Clean, we're committed to:

  • Creating powerful cleaning solutions using only natural, biodegradable ingredients
  • Maintaining a strict no-animal-testing policy across all our products
  • Supporting sustainable manufacturing practices that protect our planet
  • Providing safe, effective products that protect your family's health

Our four essential products - All-Purpose Cleaner, Eco-Friendly Bleach, Dish Soap, and Hand Soap - represent years of research and development to create the perfect balance of cleaning power and environmental responsibility.
